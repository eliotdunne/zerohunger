Marlay Park Market

Marlay Park hosts one of Dublin’s best‑known weekend markets, set in the courtyard of the historic Marlay House. It’s a large, lively market with a strong mix of food producers, crafts, and street food.

  • Fresh Irish fruit and vegetables
  • Artisan breads, pastries, and baked goods
  • Local cheeses, meats, and charcuterie
  • Street food and international hot food stalls
  • Crafts, handmade goods, and local makers
  • Family‑friendly atmosphere in a park setting

Runs every weekend, with most stalls active on Saturdays and Sundays.
