Bushy Park Market (Local to Terenure)

Bushy Park Market is the main farmers’ market serving the Terenure area. Located at the edge of Terenure and Rathfarnham, it is a popular Saturday market attracting families, locals, and food lovers.

  • Fresh Irish vegetables and fruit
  • Artisan breads, pastries, and baked goods
  • Local honey, jams, and small‑batch preserves
  • Street food and international hot food stalls
  • Craft cheeses, meats, and specialty foods
  • Handmade crafts, gifts, and local makers

Runs every Saturday, typically from late morning into the afternoon.
